
## Design System
- **Colors**: Primary indigo (#6366f1), with semantic colors for success, warning, danger, and info.
- **Typography**: Inter font, with a scale from xs to 3xl.
- **Spacing**: 0.25rem to 3rem scale.
- **Components**: Base styles for buttons, inputs, cards, and badges.
- **Dark Mode**: Supported via `prefers-color-scheme` media query.
- **Animations**: Basic fade-in, slide-in, and pulse animations.
